Larry Le | Digital Portfolio

Date: June 2022 - Present

Skills/Languages Used: HTML5, CSS, JS

I've always wanted an easy way to showcase my projects and progression throughout my time in Highschool. Welcome to the site!

What Can It Do?

One of my main goals is to make this website dynamic and fun to navigate around. Therefore, you may notice a few things such as how there are animations as you scroll down, or even a few easter eggs. This website is also mobile-responsive, allowing most people to surf around my portfolio no matter the device.

How Do You Do That?

I like to compare the Document Object Model of websites to Roblox studio: having a good understanding of parents and their children's elements really helped me make my website come to life. I also learned a lot from "CSS God" Kevin Powell, who was a big help in teaching me efficient and modern CSS techniques such as grid-box. Finally, creating a design plan benefited me as well, allowing me to stay consistent and make the most awesome site I can.

Key Takeaways

Learning web development was instrumental for me: I found I was able to express my creative nature, being able to build anything I could think of. It was the basis that gave me the passion for learning how things work, allowing me to expand to different areas of computer science, such as robotics with Java and even cybersecurity! The reason I'm able to innovate and take action started here, and without web development, I would have never gotten a foothold to grow and become my greatest self.
