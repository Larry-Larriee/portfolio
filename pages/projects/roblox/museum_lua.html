Roblox Studio: Museum of LUA

Date: July 2021

Skills & Languages Used: LUA, Roblox Studio Environment

The Museum of LUA is educational game showcasing the different properties of Roblox Studio that allow us to create amazing experiences!

What Can It Do?

This creation is uncopylocked, meaning anyone from Roblox can fork the game and look inside the program. From cloning to animation to data-saving and more, the Museum of LUA can guide and assist individuals that want to code on Roblox by revealing the different things they can learn and apply to their own game.

How Do You Do That?

I wanted to script this experience to help others learn, but also to document the personal learning that I was doing. Many these lessons came from TheDevKing, an experienced Roblox developer who I knew could help me become more knowledgeable with LUA. If there was something I didn't understand, I would also go on the Roblox DevForums and ask others about the material.

Key Takeaways

In general, Roblox Development has really taught me how to discipline myself to keep pushing past concepts that I did not totally understand at first. It's also very easy to publish your game on Roblox for anyone to play, which allowed me to get feedback from my friends on what else I should be adding to the game. Of course, my games are not perfect and there are likely still bugs, but for me, Roblox Studio was a huge step in the right direction for my personal development.
