Roblox Studio: Titan Legends

Date: M/D/Y

Skills & Languages Used: LUA, Roblox Studio Environment

Titan Legends is a multiplayer role-play game with the primary objective of growing your titan character to destory other players and dominate! Inspired from the manga Attack on Titan

What Can It Do?

Titan Legends encapsulates a variety of game features made from scratch. These are but are not limited to: a main menu, multiple fighting environments that you can access based on rebirth, optional gamepasses to allow the user to grow faster, and keybinds (PC & Mobile) to attack other players.

How Do You Do That?

As my first major game, A lot of my knowledge for programming this experience came from Mentor Roblox Developers, especially TheDevKing. I remember waking up everyday just pumped to watch another video from TheDevKing's Roblox Development playlist, such as about remote events and GUIs, and thinking of ways in which I could add some of these concepts to my own game.

Key Takeaways

In general, Roblox Development has really taught me how to discipline myself to keep pushing past concepts that I did not totally understand at first. It's also very easy to publish your game on Roblox for anyone to play, which allowed me to get feedback from my friends on what else I should be adding to the game. Of course, it's not perfect and there are still bugs, but for me, Titan Legends was a huge step in the right direction for my personal development.
