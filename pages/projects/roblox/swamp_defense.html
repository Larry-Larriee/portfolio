Roblox Studio: Swamp Defense

Date: August 2021

Skills & Languages Used: LUA, Roblox Studio Environment

Swamp Defense is an endless single-player endurance game where you defend a Flordia man's home and get rewarded for it. Go get em tiger!

What Can It Do?

Swamp Defense envelops a wide set of game features made to keep the player coming back and enjoying a quality game. Such features include: an animation main menu, a shop to upgrade weapons, a global leaderboard to compare your times with hundreds of other players, and even a little interactive tutorial that guides the user. There's also the ability for weapon auto-reload and a camera switch for a 2D or 3D style gameplay!

How Do You Do That?

I had a lot of inspiration from a childhood game called Swamp Attack, in which you also save yourself from swamp creatures. The goal was to test my skills as a Roblox developer, and this experience really challenged me to think of how I could add features like gun upgrades, gun animation, working AI, and more. I also fiddled with ways to make the game feel like it was well made, such as the interactive tutorial mentioned earlier: this is definitely one of the most fun projects I've ever done :)

Key Takeaways

In general, Roblox Development has really taught me how to discipline myself to keep pushing past concepts that I did not totally understand at first. It's also very easy to publish your game on Roblox for anyone to play, which allowed me to get feedback from my friends on what else I should be adding to the game. Of course, my games are not perfect and there are likely still bugs, but for me, Roblox Studio was a huge step in the right direction for my personal development.
