About Me

Let me tell you a bit about my journey.

December 2020

My Exploring Computer Science class would introduce me to web development for the first time, teaching me about the syntax of basic HTML and CSS.

January 2021

Creation of Critiverse as a class project

February-March 2021

I would go on to learn about scratch-level javascript and create animations like The Scratch Men Awaken

April-May 2021

Now that I knew the disiplines of programming, I begun to become more interested in my childhood game Roblox. I came interested in how different games worked and behaved and learned about Roblox Studio

June 2021

At first I felt very lost in terms of Roblox scripting. However, as I started to take free public models on Roblox, I got more used to the environment and began learning from Youtubers like Alvinblox and TheDevKing. It's difficult to do things by yourself and these people acted as essentially mentors for me.

July 2021

Creation of my first major Roblox Experience: Titan Legends. This game would undergo different env and changes as I learned more and more about things like animations, lighting, client and server events, player scripts, workspace scripts, and more.

This portfolio was started during the summer of 2022: thanks for visiting!
