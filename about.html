About Me

Hello! My name is Larry Le. I want my future to be involved in technology and computer science, the STEM field I've been interested in ever since I began my freshman year of high school.

How Did I Get Here?

The spark to my interest started in my Exploring Computer Science class, a mandatory course I had to do because of the program I was in. It may have been a blessing in disguise, because I never would have considered CS. I would eventually learn the basic skills needed to create a static website. However, I often went above and beyond the course description, constantly going on W3Schools to learn about more properties.

By the time my freshman summer rolled around, I found myself branching out beyond web design, curious to see what else I could create with CS. I ended up pursuing LUA and Roblox Development, because something in me wanted to find out more about how that shooting mechanic worked, how those zombies knew exactly how to follow me, and all the other features that made a Roblox game so cool. ðŸ˜ƒ

By my Sophomore year, I learned Python to program Discord bots, in addition to cybersecurity through a program called CyberStart. After exploring my options, I've decided that I want to specialize in full-stack development, centering my Junior year on learning how to do things like launch a server to send POST requests.

What's The Point?

The point is very simple. Pursuing full-stack development means that I can create services, whether that be on browser, desktop, or phone, to positively impact the community and leave this place better than I found it. This is all I want, and I will do everything I can to make it happen.
